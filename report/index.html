Report a Threat to the State of Michigan

The Office of State Intelligence partners with the Michigan Attorney General to receive, review, and act on reports of threats. All submissions are handled carefully and evaluated for relevance to the state’s security.

How to Submit a Threat Report

To submit a report, you must go through the Michigan Attorney General Criminal Tipline. The tipline is responsible for reviewing all incoming reports and determining whether they require attention from the Office of State Intelligence.

  • Visit the Attorney General Criminal Tipline.
  • Submit your report with as much detail as possible, including dates, locations, and involved parties.
  • The Attorney General’s office will assess the report and, if necessary, forward it to OSI for investigation.

Please note: OSI does not accept direct submissions. All threat reports must first go through the Attorney General’s Criminal Tipline for verification and prioritization.
