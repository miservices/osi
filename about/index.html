About the Office of State Intelligence

We are dedicated to safeguarding Michigan by monitoring threats, analyzing intelligence, and providing actionable insights to state leadership.

Our Mission

The Office of State Intelligence exists to protect the sovereignty, security, and critical interests of Michigan. By collecting and analyzing information on threats ranging from cyberattacks to espionage, we ensure the state can proactively address risks before they impact citizens, infrastructure, or the economy.
